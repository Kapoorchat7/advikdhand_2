Who I Am

Young author. Financial educator. Speaker.

Advik Dhand is a young author, speaker, and financial educator on a mission to teach young people what school never did — how money actually works. Inspired by his father's background in finance, Advik always questioned the "school-only" model of learning, seeking real-world lessons instead. He has authored three books and spoken at events to help the next generation take control of their financial future.

When he's not writing or speaking, Advik loves playing guitar, exploring new ideas, and finding practical ways to apply financial concepts in everyday life. His goal is simple: to empower young readers to believe they can make a difference — starting now.
